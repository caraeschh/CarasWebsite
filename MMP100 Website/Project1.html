My Process

  1. First, I picked out the two images. I picked out a photograph of my face, as well as a spring floral photo I took last year.
  2. I wanted only a bit of the flowers to peek out, so I selected and created a mask.
  3. Created the lines with white lines
  4. I then created the box, and lowered the opacity to create a more transparent look.
  5. Finally, I chose the text that I wanted to use. I settled on a quote from Jane Eyre
